About EMIT Mission


    Strong winds on one continent can whip up mineral rock dust, the airborne dust can travel travel to and affect different continents. Suspended in the air dust can heat up or cool down the atmosphere and or Earth's surface. This heating or cooling effect is the main purpose of NASA's Earth Surface Mineral Dust Source Investigation (EMIT) mission.
    Mineral dust can other effects on our planet such as forming clouds. It can also provide nutrients to ecosystems when it lands on land or water.
